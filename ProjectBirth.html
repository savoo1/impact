Project Birth

Co-Investment via SPV into a digital health start-up that uses smart tech to empower women to understand and facilitate their birth control, and is advancing towards an FDA approved over-the-counter birth control pill in the USA.

Impact profile

Key Terms

Financials

US$4.5 million (max) convertible promissory note, 6% annual interest rate payable at maturity, 31 December 2021.

The company's targeted raise is US$4.5 million, Brava Investments' special purpose vehicle (SPV) will represent $2.5million.

Brava Investments is a growth equity private equity firm investing in businesses that advance the economic equality of women.

Use of funds from the convertible note raise is to continue to move the company's licensed pill closer to over-the-counter status.

Impact

The pill is available in the majority of countries outside the US as an over-the-counter (OTC) drug, which increases access and affordability.

The company obtained the rights to a common version of the pill and is presently working through approvals from the FDA to distribute its version of the pill OTC.

The company intends to sell its version of the pill for less than 20% of the cost of currently available pill-form contraception through insurance, while maintaining gross margins of over 100%.

The lower price point and non-prescription transaction opens distribution to women who were previously unable to afford the preventative solution.

Expanding the option for planning helps girls and women complete educations, extend educations, and remain in education.
