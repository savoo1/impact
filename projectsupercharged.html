Project Supercharged

The Company has developed and patented its means of packaging, installing, and managing grid-connected energy storage. Competitive advantages of the Company's batteries include the elimination of redundant hardware costs and materials, and the reduction of unnecessary conversion efficiency losses. The company is partnering with large-cap energy and utility companies to modernize grid infrastructure for efficiency and integrate renewable energy into the global power supply, including 27% of demand coming from North America and 19% from Africa. Now through successful demonstration, the Company is executing on its commercial ramp through 2019.

Impact

Advancing the integration of renewable energy into the global power grid; BNEF projects that “renewables will produce 51% of global power… and capture 72% of the $10.2 trillion in new generation spend” by 2040.
