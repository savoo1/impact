Project Molecular

The Company is developing a connected diagnostic platform for use in the home and by healthcare professionals to enable fast and convenient access to health information through a range of simple, self-serve tests that deliver results to your mobile device in minutes. Company's objective is to be the world's provider of household and point-of-care molecular data via mobile devices. Company founded in 2010. Clinical testing to secure FDA approval started in January 2018.

Strategic partner and lead investor is a multinational medical devices and pharmaceutical company. Strategic partner (pharma giant) and venture capital firm will invest US$5 million each. Company is in advanced talks with three more pharma giants.

The company is planning to add at least 20 additional tests in the medium term. Currently has 12 US patents and first Chinese patent coming shortly.

The company is in final stage talks with a potential strategic partner that could provide up to ~US$10-$30 million in non-dilutive grant funding.

Impact
