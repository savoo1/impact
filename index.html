The
IMP ACT Alliance

The IMP/ACT ALLIANCE is a credible commitment and consortium of over 50 experienced and trusted impact entrepreneurs, investment managers, advisors and educators.

Jointly we aim to provide qualified investors with a set of easy to apply tools that support the access to and assessment of impact investment opportunities globally.

1

IMP/ACT FRAMEWORK

An easy to implement checklist on how to assess impact (criteria co-developed with experts in this field based on existing assessment frameworks such as e.g. the Impact Management Project)

2

COMMUNITY-INVESTING PLATFORM:

A digital central repository of global impact investment opportunities for qualified investors (originated and peer-selected by the partner network)
